About Me

Ever since I was young, I've been fascinated by how computers work. I started out learning the scripting language Batch, and quickly became hooked on programming. Since then, I've expanded my skills and experience with a variety of programming languages and technologies, including Rust, Go, Python, Docker, Linux, and more.

Driven by a desire to apply my expertise to real-world problems, I have contributed to several community projects. As the co-founder and primary contributor of AtlasOS, a Windows modification designed for gaming, I take pride in enhancing user experiences. Additionally, I created LiftX, a Rust-based workout tracker, and have participated in other projects aimed at optimizing computer performance. I am deeply passionate about leveraging technology to solve complex problems, and I am always eager to broaden my knowledge and take on new challenges.
